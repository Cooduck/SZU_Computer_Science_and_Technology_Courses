Shenzhen University (SZU), located in Shenzhen, Guangdong Province, is a comprehensive public university approved by the Ministry of Education of the People's Republic of China. Established in 1983, SZU was inscribed by Mr. Deng Xiaoping and has received significant attention from national leaders since its inception. The university operates under the motto "Self-reliance, Self-discipline, and Self-improvement," striving to become a high-level research and teaching institution that cultivates internationally-minded applied talents.

Situated in the scenic Nanshan District, SZU boasts a beautiful campus equipped with state-of-the-art facilities. The university comprises numerous colleges offering a wide range of undergraduate and graduate programs across disciplines such as humanities, sciences, engineering, economics, management, law, and art. SZU places a strong emphasis on international education and has established exchange partnerships with universities across the globe, providing students with extensive opportunities for learning and development.

In recent years, Shenzhen University has achieved notable success in research, particularly in cutting-edge fields like information technology, new materials, and biomedicine. The university also actively serves the local economy and society through industry-university collaboration, promoting the application of scientific research outcomes. As Shenzhen continues to grow rapidly as a special economic zone, Shenzhen University has emerged as an influential educational and research institution both nationally and internationally.
